## Verdict

```
  pass_ks          True     (uniformity preserved; combiner correct)
  pass_ledger      True     (empirical ledger-fire rate within expected)
  pass_ping        True     (empirical ping-fire rate within expected)
  overall_pass     True
```

The instrument correctly distinguishes signal from the null at the
advertised rates. The combined p-value is uniform on [0,1] under a
verified-stationary source, which means:

- The ledger threshold (`p < 0.01`) fires at ~1% under null, as advertised.
- The ping threshold (`p < 0.001`) fires at ~0.19% under null — slightly
  above theoretical 0.1% but inside the 3× pass band (observed 8/4320 vs
  ~4 expected). Well within Poisson noise at this N, and the user-facing
  rate is anyway capped at 3 pings/day/user by the dispatcher regardless
  of detector fire rate.
- Per-detector uniformity rules out a stray scaling bug in any of the
  three detector implementations.
